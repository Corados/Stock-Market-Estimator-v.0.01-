# Stock-Market-Estimator-v.0.01-
A Java-based application that analyzes and estimates stock market trends using financial APIs and mathematical models.

## Introduction
Recently, many individual investors have entered the stock market. However, many lack a deep understanding of market dynamics, often buying stocks simply based on brand recognition and panic-selling at the slightest dip, leading to regrettable losses.

I believe this happens due to a lack of accessible analysis tools. While every investor has their own strategy, technical analysis provides a data-driven perspective on market trends.

This project aims to bridge that gap by developing a program that simplifies complex technical indicators. Our goal is to provide a reliable reference for financial beginners, helping them understand potential market movements and make more informed investment decisions.
